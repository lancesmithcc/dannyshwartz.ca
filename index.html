Danny Schwartz

ARTIST • TEACHER • TRUTH SEEKER

HOME

My name is Danny Schwartz. I navigate life in the rhythm of Shamanic Folk music and find balance through the teachings of Martial Zen. As a traveler in pursuit of truth, It is my goal to marry the deep-rooted wisdom of these lineages channeling through spiritual movement routines and resonant Shamanic Folk songs from the heart.

Guided by Taoist tenets and Buddhist insights and grounded in the structure of martial forms, my offerings aim to uplift and awaken the consciousness of those I come into contact with. I intend to introduce notions of unity, self-discipline, and internal peace to those who walk this path with me. This subtly woven tapestry of art and teaching, anchored in the quest for equilibrium, is my way of serving in this world.

My aim is not to lead, but to walk alongside, encouraging a deeper spiritual connection and a more embodied practice. I hope that this journey can spark an inner awakening in those who join me, helping them discover their potential and find harmony in their lives.
