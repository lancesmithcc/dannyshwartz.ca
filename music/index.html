SHAMANIC FOLK

My music weaves the mystical spirit of shamanism into the honest, heartfelt strands of folk music. I draw a deep inspiration from the transformative, healing energies of plant medicine/teachers, channeling its sacred essence into soundscapes designed for healing, self-realization, and heightened awareness. Each melody, each introspective verse, aims to kindle a sense of connection to Mother Earth and the inherent sanctity of existence.

Every note and lyric I share is a reflection of my spiritual and my human journey is an effort to shine a light on deeper truths and subtly uplift our shared vibrational frequency. My art serves as an open invitation to others to delve into the depth of their inner realms and to perceive the intricate web of interconnectivity that binds us all. My hope is that this shared experience fosters an environment of unity and empathy, offering a tender cradle for the blossoming of individual and collective spiritual evolution.
